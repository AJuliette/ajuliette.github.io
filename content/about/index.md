Hi ! 

I'm Juliette. I'm a Ruby on Rails back-end developer. But that wasn't my initial plan to be honest ! ^^ I  have a political science degree and I used to work for non-profits which organized musical activities for kids.

I thought of learning to code, but it was simply an idea in the back of my mind. And then at the end of a job contract I jumped into it and followed a bootcamp in 2018, [The Hacking Project](https://www.thehackingproject.org/).

Well, I absolutely adored it ! I loved to code, I loved all the nerdiness about it (turns out I'm a geek, as you may have spotted with this website design) and above all I love the community. I get inspired everyday by awesome people :)
